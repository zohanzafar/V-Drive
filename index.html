About V-Drive

What is V-Drive?

V-Drive is a virtual driving system that helps people learn how to drive safely. It uses a VR setup where users can practice driving, take tests on road signs and rules, and go through real-life traffic situations all without the risks of being on the road. The system also checks how users react in different scenarios to help improve their decision-making.

Why We Built It

In Pakistan, many drivers face problems because they don’t get proper training. They often ignore traffic rules, drive carelessly, or don’t know how to handle pressure. Most driving schools only teach the basics and don’t prepare people for real challenges. This leads to accidents and unsafe roads.

We built V-Drive to change that. Our goal is to give new drivers a safer and more complete way to learn. By using virtual practice, clear lessons, and simple tests, V-Drive helps people build real skills before they ever sit behind the wheel of a car.

Our Solution

V-Drive is designed to help new drivers learn and improve in a safe and controlled way. The system is divided into three main parts, each focusing on a key area of driver training.

Rule and Sign Test

This section teaches users about road signs and traffic laws through quizzes and real-world examples. It helps build a solid understanding of rules.

Psychological Test

This section checks how users respond in different situations, helping to measure focus, reaction time, and decision-making under pressure.

Driving Test

In this section, users practice real-world driving tasks like turning, parking, and handling traffic in a virtual environment using a steering wheel and pedals.

Features

Realistic Driving Practice

Users drive in a virtual space that looks and feels like real roads, with traffic lights, turns, and other vehicles.

Rule and Sign Learning

Users go through short tests and examples to understand road signs and traffic laws.

Decision-Making Test

The system checks how users respond to different situations, like quick stops or unexpected events.

Hands-on Driving Experience

Users drive using a steering wheel and pedals, just like in a real car.

Custom Training Paths

The system can be adjusted for beginners or more experienced users based on what they need to learn or improve.

Driving Performance Report

After the session, users get a report showing what they did well and where they need more practice.

Scope and Purpose

Better Understanding of Road Rules

V-Drive helps users learn road signs and traffic laws through tests.

Improved Driving Skills

Users can safely practice tasks like turning, parking, and reversing in a virtual setting.

Safer Learning Environment

New drivers can make mistakes and learn from them without any risk of accidents or damage.

Support for Different Skill Levels

Whether someone is just starting or wants to improve, the system adjusts to their needs.

Lower Cost and Resource Use

Training in a virtual environment removes the need for fuel, real description, or physical space.

Focus on Real-World Decision Making

The system checks how users react to everyday traffic situations, helping them make better choices on the road.
